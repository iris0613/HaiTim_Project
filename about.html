Overview

As a global photography platform,

Street Photography takes the form of a digital gallery that features a collection of various digital assets. It is dedicated to supporting street photographers worldwide, collecting and showcasing their work, and fostering the medium of photography on a global scale.

The collecting artists of any age, experience level, portfolio depth or industry popularity, the site aims to collect photographers and their exciting work from all corners of the world for its audience. In addition, it will provide access to people around the world who are interested in photographic culture, street black and white photography, humanities, art, etc...

History

Street photography, a genre of photography that records everyday life in a public place. The very publicness of the setting enables the photographer to take candid pictures of strangers, often without their knowledge. Street photographers do not necessarily have a social purpose in mind, but they prefer to isolate and capture moments which might otherwise go unnoticed

Street photography gained momentum from the late 19th century to the early 1970s. With the advent of the portable camera during this period, photographers were able to shoot more easily in public places. Modern technology and media developments have also provided more technical support for street photography.
